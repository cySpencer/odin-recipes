Strawberry Banana Protein Smoothie

Description

A protein smoothie great for a meal placement or after a workout. This protein consists banana, strawberries, almonds, water, ice cubes and protein powder. A good refreshing beverage for the summer.

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 1/4 cups sliced strawberries
  • 10 whole almonds
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate flavored protein powder

Instructions

  1. Place the banana, strawberries, almonds, and water into a blender.
  2. Blend to mix, then add ice cubes and puree until smooth.
  3. Add the protein powder and continue mixing until evenly distributed for about 30 second.
